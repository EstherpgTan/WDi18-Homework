Ada Lovelace
  • Born as Augusta Ada Byron in London, on December 10, 1815.
  • Daughter of famous poet, Lord Byron.
  • A mathematician and worked closely with Charles Babbage (known as "father of computers" and fellow British mathematician).
  • She is well known for her work on Charles Babbage's early mechanical general-purpose computer (the Analytical Engine) which led to her famous note on her work on engine (the first algorithm).
  • She is often considered to be the first programmer.
  • In November 27, 1852, she died of uterine cancer and buried next to her father in the graveyard of Church of St. Mary Magdelene in Nottingham, England.
