The difference machine
  • Ada Lovelace had a close working relationship with Charles Babbage, who is also her mentor, on the analytical engine.
  • She had helped also translated the work of Italian engineer, Luigi Menabrea, which she also supplemented an elaborated set of notes.
  • The notes, many would consider to be the first computer program, contained an algorithm designed to be carried out by a machine.
  • Ada's Notes to the translation of Menabrea's memoirs terminated with a step-by-step description of how 'the engine could compute the Numbers of Bernoulli, this being (in the form in which we shall deduce it) a rather complicated example of its powers.
